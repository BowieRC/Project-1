About Us

More than a Recipe App

The Supprt Your Local app is where culinary creativity meets community sustainability. We are more than just a recipe app; we're a passionate advocate for local agriculture.

We believe in the power of ethically sourced ingredients to elevate your homemade dishes. Our mission is simple: to connect you with the bountiful harvest of local farmers while supporting the growth of our vibrant agricultural community.

You'll not only discover mouthwatering recipes but also have the opportunity to support your local farmers and growers while doing so. We're committed to reducing food miles, promoting sustainable farming practices, and celebrating the incredible flavours that can only be found in the freshest, farm-to-table produce.
